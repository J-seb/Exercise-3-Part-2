Homemade Pizza

Hi, this time I brought to you an incredible homemade pizza to cook in a regular oven. It's an economic recipe, easy to cook and so delicious. With a homemade pizza you can share with your family and friends and get a great experience. So lets jump right there!

Ingredients

The Dough

  • 1kg of flour (this flour can be a mix of different cereals)
  • 650ml of water
  • 3 tablespoon of cooking oil
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 10gr of yeast

The Pizza

  • 300 ml of tomato pulp
  • 0.5kg of Mozzarella
  • Another ingredient you like

Preparation

First you have to do the dough, mix in a bowl the flour with the salt and the yeast with a spoon. Then incorporate slowly a half of the water and mix it. Later put the other half into the bowl and repeat the mix. When all ingredients are incorporate, you must to start to knead the dough with your hands. Add the cooking oil to the dough and continue the process of knead.

Finally put the dough inside the bowl, close it, and let it rest for 2 hours, this is necesary because you need the dough get brew

Meanwhile you can prepare the other ingredients, you can do a mix of different ingredients like ground beef, chicken, mushrooms, salami, pineapple (I really don't recommend it), etc. And preheat the oven

When the dough is ready you can see it grown. cut the dough in some pieces and extend in a plate, the plate previously must have some oil, then add on the dough the tomato pulp, and put it into the oven for 20 minutes.

Finally add the other ingredients, mainly the mozzarella, and put the plate into the oven again for 10 minutes.

Enjoy it !!!
